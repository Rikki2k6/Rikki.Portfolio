From Circuits to Intelligence

Where Engineering Meets Expression

It started with electrical and computer engineering — logic gates, circuit diagrams, the language of electrons, dsa, coding. But somewhere between the labs and the lectures, I found myself drawn to a different kind of current: the flow of ideas.

Photography taught me to slow down and observe. Theatre taught me to communicate without filters. And AI is teaching me that the most powerful tool is one that amplifies human creativity, not replaces it.

Today I sit at the intersection of technology, design, and storytelling — exploring how these worlds can speak the same language.
